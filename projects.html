STEM Egypt Hack Club is a collection of talented student coders and engineers, and this page includes projects our members made both within and outside of the club, on teams and individually.


Calculator

Calculator is a well made software that offers both basic and complex mathematical operations. It carries out scientific operations including trigonometric, logarithmic, and exponential functions as well as fundamental computations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Image-text Editor

The main features allow you to rapidly add text to photographs, alter the text's size, colour, and font, rotate and resize it, apply text effects, add emojis and stickers, and do a lot more.

Candy Catch

A handful of cards with a variety of sweet images and easily visible goals! The object of the game is to count the candies that have the same shape or colour as many times as the number shown on the card, and to do it as quickly as possible. A portable game for everyone's enjoyment!
